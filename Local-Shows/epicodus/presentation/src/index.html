What is Local Shows?


A discovery platform for local shows playing near you

A new way to discover music

A place to store upcoming shows

And a place to keep track of shows you've been to

Finding shows

From the home page you'll see a list of shows within 10 miles of your area


To find more information about a show just hover over it


for even more information click on the artists name to go to the ticket page


For more specific results try out the filter menu


Saved shows

From the shows page you'll see a list of all of your saved shows


Or you can add a custom show

with live preview
